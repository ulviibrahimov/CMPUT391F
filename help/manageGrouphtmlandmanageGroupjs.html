This page is used for the updating and editing of existing groups.


Updating and editing of group is limited to the user who owns the group and the 'admin' user.


Upon sending any request to update the group, the logged in user is validated to ensure they


have permission.


This page allows the owner to:


-Change the group name


-Disband (delete) the group


-Add new members


-Remove members


-Transfer group ownership to other member
