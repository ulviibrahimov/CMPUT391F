To edit an image's information:


·        Login first.


·        Click "View Images" in the page header.


·        Click "My Images" on the new page.


·        Select the image you wish to edit.


·        At the bottom of the page there will be a link to "Edit", click it.


o   If the link isn't present you do not have permission to edit the image.


·        Edit the desired fields (Permission/group, subject, date taken, location, description)


·        Hit Save


·        Your changes will either be saved or an error message will be displayed.
