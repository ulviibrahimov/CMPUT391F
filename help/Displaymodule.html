This module is used to display a list of thumbnail images on the screen for a given query such that clicking a thumbnail will lead to the display of the corresponding image and descriptive information such as the subject, place, etc. Furthermore, this module will also be used by a client to update the descriptive/security information for any image.

For example, by clicking somewhere associated with a thumbnail, the client will be able to update the descriptive and/or security information associated with the image, assuming that the client is indeed the owner of the image.
