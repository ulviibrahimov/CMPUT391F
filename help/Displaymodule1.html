After you login, when you click on the View Images in the header, it will display a list of browse type for the images.

My Images will take you to the page with all your private images. Public Images will take you to the page with all the public images. Group Images will take you to the page with all your group members' group images. And Popular Images will take you to the page with five most popular images. Any browse type has a return link, you can click on return and return to the previous page.

When you browse images in any browse type, you can click on any images to view the full size image and its descriptions. The return at the bottom can bring you back to your previous browsing.

If you are the author of this image, there will be an edit link that allow you to edit the description of this image.
