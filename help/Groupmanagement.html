To create a group:


·        Login first


·        Click "Groups" in the page header


·        Click "Create New"


·        Enter a group name and notice (optional)


·        Hit "Create!"


·        The group will either be created or an error message will be displayed.



To leave a group:


·        Login first


·        Click "Groups" in the page header


·        Click the "Leave" button beside the group you wish to leave.


o   "Leave" will only appear beside groups you belong to but do not own.  (Groups listed under "Groups I Belong to").  If you wish to leave a group you own, either first transfer leadership of the group, or disband (delete) the group.


·        You will either successfully leave the group or an error message will be displayed.



To delete a group:


·        Login first


·        Click "Groups" in the page header


·        Hit the "Manage" button beside the group you wish to delete.


o   "Manage" will only appear beside groups you own.  (Groups listed under "My Groups")


·        Hit the "Disband" button.


·        Confirm with "Yes"


·        The group will either be deleted or an error message will be displayed.  If successful you will be redirected to the groups page because the group no longer exists.



To transfer group leadership:


·        Login first


·        Click "Groups" in the page header


·        Hit the "Manage" button beside the group you wish to delete.


o   "Manage" will only appear beside groups you own.  (Groups listed under "My Groups")


·        Hit the "Transfer Leadership" button.


o   "Transfer Leadership" will only appear beside other group members that aren't you.  They are listed under "Members".


o   If there are no members please add one first.


·        Confirm with "Yes"


·        The group's leadership will either be transferred to this member or an error message will be displayed.  If successful you will be redirected to the "Groups" page because you no longer have permission to manage this group.



To add a member:


·        Login first


·        Click "Groups" in the page header


·        Hit the "Manage" button beside the group you wish to delete.


o   "Manage" will only appear beside groups you own.  (Groups listed under "My Groups")


·        Enter a username in the "New Member" text box located to the left of the "Add Memer!" button.


·        (optional) Enter a notice in the "New Member Notice" text box located just below the "Add Member!" button.


·        Hit the "Add Member!" button.


·        The member will either be added to the group or an error message will be displayed.  If successful, the "Members" list will be updated.



To remove a member:


·        Login first


·        Click "Groups" in the page header


·        Hit the "Manage" button beside the group you wish to delete.


o   "Manage" will only appear beside groups you own.  (Groups listed under "My Groups")


·        Find the user you want to remove in the "Members" list.


o   You can not remove your self.


·        Hit the "remove" button.


·        Confirm with "Yes".


·        The member will either be removed from the group or an error message will be displayed.  If successful, the "Members" list will be updated.



To change a group's name:


·        Login first


·        Click "Groups" in the page header


·        Hit the "Manage" button beside the group you wish to delete.


o   "Manage" will only appear beside groups you own.  (Groups listed under "My Groups")


·        Fill in the "New Group Name" text box located to the left of the "Change" button.


·        Hit the "Change" button.


·        The member will either be removed from the group or an error message will be displayed.  If successful, a success message will be displayed and the group title will be updated.
